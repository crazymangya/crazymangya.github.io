Machinima on The Elder Scrolls V - Skyrim:

This was a team project.

The team members and their roles were as follows:

  1. Manas Murali - Story and Sound Artist
  2. Mangesh Thombare - Video capture & editing
  3. Akash Agarwal - Storyboarding & Poster

This is the full video of Machinima.

The objective of the project was to create a 3-4 minute long funny video using the footage from the Game The Elder scrolls V : SKYRIM.

The purpose of the Machinima project was learning the skills of storytelling and basic scripting skills. We also worked on Sound design for the Machinima.
